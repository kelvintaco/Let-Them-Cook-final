Caldereta

Indulge in the rich and savory experience of Kaldereta! Uncover the secrets of preparing this Filipino classic as you master the art of tenderizing meat, blending savory tomato sauce, and infusing a medley of flavors. Follow our step-by-step guide to bring the warmth and heartiness of Kaldereta to your table. Elevate your culinary prowess and savor the delicious taste of tradition.

prep time

15-20 mins.

cook time

2-3hrs.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Beef Lunch Intermediate

instructions

step 1

Heat oil in a pot. Fry the carrots for 2 minutes. Make sure that all sides are cooked equally. Remove from the pot. Set aside.

step 2

Fry the potato until the color of the outer part turns light brown. Remove from the pot and set aside.

step 3

Using the remaining oil, sauté garlic, onion, and thyme. Continue sautéing until the onion caramelizes.

step 4

Add beef short ribs. Cook until the color of the outer part turns light brown.

step 5

Pour tomato sauce and red wine. Add Knorr Beef Cube.

step 6

Add soy sauce and water. Cover the pot. Continue cooking the stew in low heat setting until the beef tenderizes completely.

step 7

Add liver spread and bell pepper. Cook for 3 minutes.

step 8

Add carrot and potato. Cook for 3 minutes.

step 9

Add cheddar cheese and then season with salt and ground black pepper.

ingredients

4 lbs beef short ribs

1 Knorr Beef Cube

14 ounces tomato sauce

1 cup red wine

2 large potato

1 large carrot

1 cup manzanilla olives

2 bell pepper sliced

4 sprigs thyme

2 large onion

1 head garlic chopped

3 tablespoons soy sauce

½ cup liver spread

1 cup water

½ cup liver spread

salt and ground black pepper to taste

tools

Knife and Chopping Board for Vegetables

Large Heavy Pot With Lid

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups

Pressure Cooker
