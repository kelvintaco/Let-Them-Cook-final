Lumpia

Learn how to cook sizzling pork sisig. Elevate this favorite Filipino dish with Lady's Choice Real Mayonnaise. Try this easy-to-prepare recipe now.

prep time

5 mins.

cook time

15 mins.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Pork Occasion Easy

instructions

step 1

Combine ground pork, singkamas, carrots, green onions and celery in a medium bowl. Then, add oyster sauce, salt and pepper, sesame oil and 3 Tbsp of Ladyâ€™s Choice Mayonnaise; mix well.

step 2

Spoon about 1 tablespoon of the mixture into each lumpia wrapper. Roll tightly. Seal ends of the wrapper with water. Deep fry until golden brown. Serve hot.

ingredients

1/2 kilogram ground pork

1/2 cup singkamas, finely chopped

1/4 cup carrots, finely chopped

1/4 cup green onions, finely chopped

1/8 cup celery (equiv. to 2 Tbsp)

1 tbsp oyster sauce

1/2 tsp ground pepper

2 tsps sesame oil

3 tbsps Lady's Choice mayonnaise

lumpia wrappers

cooking oil for deep frying

tools

Knife

Stainless Tray
