Pastil

Pastil is a traditional Filipino dish that originated in the Maguindanao region of Mindanao. It is a flavorful and convenient meal made by wrapping seasoned rice and meat in banana leaves and then steaming or boiling the packets until cooked. The dish is commonly associated with the Tausug people and is often eaten as a quick and portable snack or meal.

prep time

15-20 mins.

cook time

30-60mins

serving

4 servings

Tags : Chicken Lunch easy

instructions

step 1

In a pot add 1 liter of water, 1 tsp Salt and 1 tsp Black Pepper & 2 Laurel Leaves Add 350g Chicken Breast Fillet Boil on med. heat for about 15 mins. Drain, let it cool down & shred the chicken

step 2

In a fry pan pour ½ cup Oil

step 3

Add minced Garlic and minced Red Onion

step 4

Fry until translucent then add 350g shredded chicken and saute

step 5

Add ½tsp Black Pepper, ½tsp Turmeric & 1½tbsp Sugar

step 6

Add 4 tbsp soy sauce & saute

step 7

Add 3 tbsp vinegar & let it simmer

step 8

add 2 tbsp margarine (optional)

step 9

Fry until chicken flakes are crispy

ingredients

½ cup Canola Oil

2 cloves Garlic minced

1 med. Red Onion minced

350 g Chicken boiled & Shredded

4 tbsp Soy Sauce

3 tbsp White Vinegar

1½ tbsp White/Brown Sugar

½ tsp Black Pepper

½ tsp Turmeric Powder optional

2 tbsp Margarine optional

tools

Cooking pot or steamer

Cooking utensils

Measuring tools

Cutting board

Mixing bowls
