Pork Humba

The Pork Humba it is Filipino Dish was originally made in Visayas Region. It is perfect from any side dish like vegetables and a bowl of rice.

prep time

15 mins.

cook time

40mins

serving

4 servings

Tags : Pork Lunch Dinner Intermediate

Procedure

step 1

First, combined the ingredients such as soy sause, vinegar, salt and pepper in a piece of bowl

step 2

Heat in a pan on a stove and add the marinate pork and simmer and stir in a normal heat until brown and adding the star anise and brown or red sugar.

step 3

Cover in pan and let it boil in 25 minutes(add 1 cup of water if necessary.) and until the boil is absorbed the marinated sauce and set in oil(or butter as alternate.)

step 4

Transfer to a serving dish and ready to serve.

ingredients

1 pound of Pork(e.g. pork belly in cube size)

3 pieces of garlic(chopped or minced)

3 tablespoon of Soy Sause

2 tablespoon of Vinegar

2 to 3 tablespoon of Brown or Red Sugar

Oil or Butter

2 to 3 pieces of star anise

1 cup of Water

Salt and Pepper to taste.

tools

Cooking pan

Cooking utensils

Cooking knifes

Chopping board

Mixing bowls
